Bonjour Seniors

A big fat congratulations on making it through the great four years of you life, engineering. In These years you might have made lots of memories, that you will cherish, friends who you will miss and the uncountable moments that will always stay close to your heart. But this small family of yours, the family you have built, will miss you the most. Club Excel will thrive as a legacy you have left behind and may touch peaks. But the void of eachone of you will remain hollow forever. We did not even get a chance to say a proper goodbye. Not a single chance to get a group picture. And make one last memory with each of us. Maybe that meant something, maybe you weren't ever destined to part away from the club, or the family that loves you much. We know we couldn't do the slightest of what we had planned for you. But to do nothing, you never taught us that. This is a very small token of the infinite love we have for you.
